History

A brief overview of the evolution of Lando

Goth Core!

    First inklings of standardized local dev happen while at Gotham City Drupal with Goth Core. Goth Core is using Puppet and Apache.

Kalastack

At Kalamuna, Kalastack is born, a Puppet/Vagrant based LEMP stack, the first iteration that directly targets Pantheon with specific integrations.

Share it with the world!

Kalamuna open sources their internal tool is a full blown MacOS app called Kalastack. This is still pre-docker and only for OSX.

Kalabox 2

Kalamuna goes whole hog on Docker and cross platform conquest with the launch of a Kickstarter to build Kalabox. Pantheon is a fairly avid supporter due to integration

Hard Times

Kalabox is pretty amazing! However it's long development time causes it to miss the mark

Learning and Vindication

After splitting from Kalamuna, Tandem takes what they learned from Kalabox and starts covert ops on Lando. In just a few months, Lando use explodes and quickly surpasses Kalabox usage numbers
